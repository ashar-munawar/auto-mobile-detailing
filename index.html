Frequently Asked Questions

What is car detailing?

Car detailing is a top-to-bottom thorough cleaning of your vehicle using specialized tools and products. It involves both interior and exterior cleaning, reconditioning, and finishing to restore the paintwork and interior.

How often should I get my car detailed?

We recommend a full detail every 4-6 months to maintain your vehicle's value and appearance. Simple washes should be done every 2 weeks.

What's included in a full detailing service?

It includes exterior wash, clay bar treatment, polishing/waxing, tire cleaning, interior vacuuming, shampooing of carpets/seats, dashboard cleaning, and window cleaning.
